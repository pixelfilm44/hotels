HOTELS

Build hotels. Charge outrageous rates. Bankrupt your friends.

LOBBY

Room code:

2–4 players. Share the code or link with friends, or add bots.

How to play

Goal: be the last player who isn't bankrupt.

Your turn: roll the die and drive that many squares (you skip over occupied squares). Rolling a 6 gives an extra turn.

Buying land: land on a square next to an unowned hotel plot to buy its deed (one deed per turn; you can build on it from your next turn). Land on a square next to an opponent's unbuilt land and you may force-buy it at half price.

Building: land on a PLANNING square, pick one of your sites and how many stages to add, then roll the planning die: ● pay & build, H build FREE, ×2 pay double, ● denied.

Leisure facility: once a hotel is fully built you can add its facility at the start of any of your turns — no permission needed. It raises the nightly rate.

Entrances: each time you pass CITY HALL you may buy one entrance per built hotel, placed on a free square beside it. FREE DOOR squares give one free entrance.

Contested squares (⚔): some squares sit between two hotels facing each other across the road. Only one entrance ever fits on a square — these are marked with a split-colour diamond. If your rival builds there first, you can't, so it's a race to claim the spot.

Getting paid: when an opponent lands on your entrance they roll the die for the number of nights and pay nights × nightly rate.

Bank: collect $2,000 every time you pass the bank (3+ player games only).

Debt: if you can't pay, you must auction hotels (sold complete with buildings and entrances) to the highest bidder. Out of assets and cash? You're bankrupt.
